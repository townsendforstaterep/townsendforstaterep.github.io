My name is Jim Townsend and I am the Democratic nominee for the Kentucky State House in District 12 which includes all of McLean and Webster counties and part of Daviess and Hopkins counties.

I served as Webster County Judge/Executive from 1992 until June 30th of this year when I retired to run for State Representative. Prior to that, I was a Webster County Magistrate from 1982 to 1992. I graduated from Henderson Community College with a degree in Agriculture from Murray State University. From there, I farmed for over thirty years where I learned the value of hard work. I was also the first person to graduate from Webster County High School who came back to teach there.

My wife, Carolyn, and I are residents of Dixon, KY where she is the Mayor. We are blessed with two children and five grandchildren. My family shares my love and admiration for Western Kentucky and all the hard working families that make our community a great place to live and raise a family.

It’s time to restore honesty and integrity to Frankfort, the people of Kentucky deserve as much.

I humbly ask for your support to provide the people of the 12th District with real leadership they can trust.

James R. “Jim” Townsend
